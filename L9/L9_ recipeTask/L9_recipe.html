Beetle Juice

Ingredients

  1. Appachalian Beetles
  2. Mosquito Blood
  3. Crocodile Tears
  4. Spider Web

Directions

  1. Pound the beetles in a pestle and motar grinder
  2. Mix in the beetle ground, mosquito blood, crocodile tears into a bowl
  3. shape the spider web into the form of a sphere
  4. Pour the mixture into the sphere made of spider web
  5. Leave the sphere to set in the fridge till the next new moon
  6. Beetlejuice is ready to serve!
